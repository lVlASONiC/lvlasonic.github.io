United States Air Force Aircraft

A reference guide to the fighters, bombers, surveillance aircraft, and airlifters that make up the modern Air Force fleet.

This wiki documents active U.S. Air Force aircraft, with a focus on platforms tested and operated at Eglin Air Force Base. Each entry covers the aircraft's history, role, and key specifications.
